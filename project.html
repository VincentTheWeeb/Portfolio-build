Digital Seat Media: Home Page

This is a redesign of the Digital Seat Media home page.

Case Study: Digital Seat Media

As an intern with Digital Seat Media, one of my first projects was to redesign the home page of our platform. It was an exciting opportunity for me to contribute to a company that is changing the way fans engage with live events. Our mobile web-based platform allows fans to interact with their favorite brands and fellow fans, all through a simple scan of a QR code-enabled tag located on their seat.

Functionality

To ensure that we continue to offer the best possible experience for our users, I recently created a redesign of our home page. The redesign features a clean design, with improved navigation and streamlined access to our platform's key features. The updated design also places a stronger emphasis on our partnerships with top brands, including Pizza Hut, Cheeze-It's, Braum's, AT&T, In-N-Out Burger, Chick-Fil-A, and many more.

Technologies Used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was determining what was important to highlight for our investors and future customers. We wanted to showcase the strength of our brand partnerships and the ease of use of our platform, while also highlighting the benefits of our fan engagement technology.


Another challenge I faced was my own inexperience with UI design. I spent hours refactoring my code and tweaking the layout of the page, trying to find the perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality. Looking back, I realize that I probably spent way too much time on small details, but it was a valuable learning experience for me.

Conclusion

Despite the challenges, I'm proud of what I was able to accomplish with the redesign of the home page. I was able to create a design that effectively communicated our brand message and the benefits of our platform, while also creating an engaging and intuitive user experience. And, of course, I learned to be a little less precious about my code and to prioritize functionality over perfection!
